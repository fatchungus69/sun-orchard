Our Story

Rooted in something real.

👨‍🌾
Jason Ma
Founder & Chief Cashew Officer
"Why can't something taste delicious and be good for you?"

Health nut. Flavor obsessive. The guy who got tired of compromising — so he built something better.

You know that saying: "The healthier something is, the worse it tastes"? Jason refused to accept it. As someone who took his health seriously but also genuinely loved food, he spent years searching for a snack that didn't make him choose.

He came up empty. So he did what any slightly unreasonable person with a vision does — he decided to grow it himself.

🌏 Deep in Cambodia, where the equatorial sun blazes stronger and natural spring water flows freely, Jason found the perfect conditions for something extraordinary.

The result? Cashews that grow nearly 2× larger than what you'd find anywhere else — buttery, crispy, and so umami-rich they're genuinely hard to put down. Each batch is lightly roasted to crisp up the skin just right.

One bite and you'll understand why we don't touch them with pesticides, artificial flavors, or shortcuts of any kind. We're zero-pesticide, certified organic, and fiercely committed to keeping it that way — no matter how much we grow.
