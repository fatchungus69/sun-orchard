Our Story

The cashew
deserves better.

We’ve been to plenty of grocery stores. We’ve raided the snack bowls at family gatherings. Same verdict every time: pecans go fast, almonds disappear, and the cashews… just sit there.

Can’t even be mad. Most cashews are pale, bland, and forgettable. They’ve been the afterthought of the nut aisle forever.

My dad grows cashews on a small farm in Cambodia. Five-plus years, no pesticides, no shortcuts — just natural spring water and equatorial sun. What comes off his farm doesn’t belong in a mixed bag. It’s fat, crispy, buttery, and absurdly big. Big a** cashews.

We figured the world deserved to find out. CHONK exists because this nut has been sleeping on its potential long enough. Not the leftovers. The main event.

The Difference You Can Taste

Skin-on.
The way it was meant to be.

Most cashews are blanched and stripped before roasting. You lose the skin, you lose the texture, you lose the fiber. We roast ours whole — so every bite has that signature crunch, a deeper roasted flavor, and a natural fiber boost most snacks can’t touch.
